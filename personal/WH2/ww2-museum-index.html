Welcome to the Museum

World War II was a global conflict that changed politics, society, and daily life across the world. This digital museum is organized into separate pages so each topic can answer its own essential question through explanation, artifacts, captions, and source links.

Page 1

Explains the long-term and short-term causes of World War II.

Page 2

Introduces the Allied and Axis powers and explains why they fought.

Page 3

Examines the major leaders of the war and their roles.

Page 4

Shows how the war changed civilian life on the homefront.

How to Use This Site

  • Use the navigation bar at the top to move between pages.
  • Each page includes artifact captions that connect directly to the essential question.
  • Each artifact ends with a source link, which matches your assignment instructions.
